About Us

Innovate, Inspire, Impact.

Welcome to TechLads Foundation, a platform where vision meets innovation, empowering women in technology and redefining the industry. Since 2014 , we have fostered a thriving community and built partnerships with world-class organizations to provide women with the tools, skills, and opportunities to succeed in tech. In 2022 we transform our platform even bigger to provide more reachable facilities for digital literacy program. Let Unleash your potential and embrace innovation together. Join the movement empowering Indonesian women to inspire change and drive impactful tech solution

Innovating with Indonesia's Women.

At the heart of our mission is a deep-rooted belief in the transformative power of technology and its ability to shape futures for Indonesian women. We are passionately committed to breaking down barriers and creating pathways for women to thrive in the ever-evolving digital world. Drawing inspiration from the unique stories and talents across our diverse archipelago, we provide cutting-edge training, resources, and community support to empower women as leaders and innovators in tech. Together, we're not just imagining a future where gender equality is realized in technology; we're building it, one visionary at a time.
